About This App

This app was designed and developed by Spatial Information System Lab at San Diego Supercomputer Center for the NSF Earthcube Community Inventory of EarthCube Resources for Geosciences Interoperability project.

The ECOGEO Resource Catalog is a collaboration between the ECOGEO Research Coordination Network project and the CINERGI (Community Inventory of EarthCube Resources for Geoscience Interoperability) projects. It is a community-curated registry of information resources used in geo-microbiology, genomics and related fields
